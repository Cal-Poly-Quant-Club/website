The Cal Poly Quant Club is a dynamic student-led community at California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo, dedicated to delving into the fascinating world of quantitative finance. We offer a blend of theoretical learning and practical experience through workshops, guest lectures, and hands-on projects. Our goal is to equip our members with the necessary skills to excel in this fast-paced field, bridging finance and technology to shape the future of investment.
